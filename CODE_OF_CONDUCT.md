# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to keeping an open, respectful and welcoming environment
for all participants, regardless of experience level, background, identity
or any other personal characteristic.

## Expected behavior

- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Accept constructive criticism with openness.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Show empathy toward other members.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Offensive language or imagery, personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an
[issue](https://github.com/NullSablex/rust-samp/issues) or contacting the
maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
with discretion.

The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit or reject comments,
commits, issues and other contributions that are not aligned with this
code of conduct.
